17. Commitment to Removing Reserved Profiles Upon Request

 

The initial developers of the BitClout blockchain reserved a subset of Twitter users' profiles early on in order to prevent squatting and impersonation.

 

This being said, any user with a reserved profile who would like to have their profile removed from bitclout.com can request this removal by messaging the @bitclout handle on Twitter. This is needed in order to authenticate the owner of the Twitter handle corresponding to the reserved profile. bitclout.com agrees to remove the reserved profile of any user who messages @bitclout with the corresponding Twitter account.
